⚖️ What's In Your Control vs. What's Not

It helps to know which parts of this process you can move along and which parts just take time.

In Your Control

  • ■ Completing your application promptly
  • ■ Starting coursework right away (don't wait for background check)
  • ■ Finishing certification within 5 days
  • ■ Checking the ShyftOff App & email daily
  • ■ Completing pre-production requirements within 3 days
  • ■ Asking for help in the right Rocket Relay rooms

Not in Your Control

  • ■ Background check processing time
  • ■ When NationsBenefits issues your credentials (up to 7 days)
  • ■ System access activation timing
  • ■ When I Work invite timing
  • ■ Navigation Meeting scheduling

📖 Glossary

Opt In

When you sign up for the NationsBenefits campaign within ShyftOff.

Background Check

A required screening processed by Yardstik. Does not need to be completed before starting coursework.

Certification Course

The main training course you complete to learn how to handle NationsBenefits calls. Completing this triggers your credential request and places you on the list for credentials.

Credential Request

The process where your NationsBenefits login is requested after you complete certification.

Credentials

Your NationsBenefits username and password — used to log into their systems.

Pre-Production

The phase after receiving credentials where you complete the Pre-Production course (your primary setup guide), Litmos trainings, and system access checks.

Pre-Production Course

Your primary guide for setting up all the NationsBenefits systems and tools you'll use.

Navigation Meeting

A required guided session that walks you through the NationsBenefits systems. Must be completed after Pre-Production before moving to Production.

Production

You're live and actively taking calls for NationsBenefits.

When I Work

The scheduling app where you view and pick up available shifts. Sign in at wheniwork.com or through the mobile app using the email your invite was sent to. NationsBenefits hours typically appear 1–2 business days after your Production move.

Litmos

The platform where you complete security, compliance, and additional training courses.

Rocket Relay

The messaging app where you communicate with the ShyftOff team and other agents. You'll see rooms for Certification, Pre-Production, and Production in the NationsBenefits campaign.

Ready to Learn (RTL)

A status that means you're eligible and ready to begin coursework.

Island

The secure browser required for accessing NationsBenefits systems. You'll install it during Pre-Production and use it daily in Production. GCBA must be installed and running for Island to pass its security checks.

Genesys / GCBA

The call platform you use to take NationsBenefits calls. GCBA (Genesys Cloud Browser App) must be installed and running on your device. You access Genesys through the Island browser and set your status to Available / On Queue to receive calls.

Nesting

The brief transition period between completing training and full Production. During nesting, your performance is monitored closely. You leave nesting when Litmos is 100% complete, tech is confirmed working, and early performance is acceptable.
